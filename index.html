    About Us

    The vision of C & O by Konko is to bring joy, ease, and purpose into every space. From the kitchen to the closet, and never excluding the garage, functionality is achieved through understanding the client’s desires, assessing the area(s), and utilizing the skill set of the professional(s). Whether making a small tweak to perfect a particular area or transforming each room, space by space, cleaning, organizing, and/or decluttering are available to our clients and are tailored to their specific needs.
